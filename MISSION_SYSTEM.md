# Mission System Guide

## Overview
The mission system allows players to accept missions at starbases and stations throughout the galaxy. Completing missions is the primary way to earn reputation points, which are required to requisition better ships.

## Accessing Missions

### Where to Find Missions
- Missions are available at any Federation starbase or station
- Navigate to a system with a station and select "Explore System" from the main menu
- Choose "Dock at Station" and then select "Mission Board"

### Mission Board Interface
The mission board displays:
- **Active Missions**: Currently accepted missions (max 3)
- **Available Missions**: New missions you can accept
- Mission details including difficulty, rewards, and requirements

## Mission Mechanics

### Accepting Missions
1. View available missions on the mission board
2. Check if you meet the skill requirements
3. You can have up to 3 active missions at once
4. Select a mission to accept it

### Skill Requirements
- Each mission requires a specific skill at a minimum level
- Your character's skill must meet or exceed the requirement
- Skills are: Command, Tactical, Science, Engineering, Diplomacy
- The game allows accepting missions up to 20 points below your skill level for challenge

### Completing Missions
1. Return to any station with an active mission
2. Select "Complete Active Mission" from the mission board
3. The game performs a skill check based on your skill level

### Success Calculation
Success chance is determined by your skill level vs. requirement:
- **20+ above requirement**: 95% success
- **10-19 above**: 85% success
- **0-9 above**: 75% success
- **0-10 below**: 60% success
- **11+ below**: 40% success

### Mission Rewards

#### Success
- **Full Rewards**: Reputation, Experience, and Dilithium
- **Critical Success** (15% chance): 50% bonus reputation
- **Time**: Mission duration passes (3-7 days)

#### Failure
- **Partial Rewards**: 25% reputation, 33% experience
- **No Dilithium**: Failed missions don't award dilithium
- **Time**: Mission duration still passes

### Additional Rewards
All missions include:
- Base reputation (15-100 based on difficulty)
- Experience points (20-90 based on difficulty)
- Dilithium crystals (50-150 random amount)
- Experience goes toward the relevant skill

## Strategy Tips

### Starting Out (Miranda-class)
- Focus on Easy missions (Patrol, Escort, Survey)
- Build up Tactical or Science skills
- Earn 150-200 reputation for your first ship upgrade
- Miranda starts all skills at 40-50 range

### Mid-Career (Constellation to Excelsior)
- Progress to Medium and Hard missions
- Diversify skill development
- Save reputation for major ship upgrades
- Constellation class requires 500 rep, Excelsior 2000 rep

### Advanced Career (Ambassador and above)
- Take on Very Hard and Extreme missions
- Focus on high-reputation missions
- Required: 3500+ rep for Ambassador, 8000+ for Sovereign
- Ensure high skill levels (75-80+) for best success rates

### Skill Building
- **Tactical**: Combat operations, patrols, border defense
- **Science**: Surveys, exploration, temporal investigations
- **Diplomacy**: Diplomatic missions, first contact
- **Command**: Rescue, crisis response, escort
- **Engineering**: Less common but useful for ship operations

### Maximizing Reputation
1. **Stack Missions**: Accept 3 missions you can complete
2. **Choose High Rewards**: Focus on difficulty you can handle
3. **Skill Match**: Pick missions matching your strongest skills
4. **Critical Success**: Higher skills = more crits = bonus rep
5. **Mix Sources**: Combine missions with exploration and combat

### Time Management
- Mission duration: 3-7 days
- Plan routes between stations
- Complete missions near your travel path
- Refresh mission board if options are poor

## Mission Board Operations

### Refreshing Missions
- Select "Refresh Mission Board" to generate new missions
- Free to use as often as needed
- Generates 5 random missions based on your level
- Nearby systems used as mission locations

### Mission Limits
- **Active Mission Cap**: 3 missions maximum
- Complete or fail missions to free slots
- No limit on total completed missions
- Mission counter tracks all missions generated

### Mission Locations
- Missions send you to nearby systems
- Location names appear in mission descriptions
- No requirement to physically travel there
- Automatic completion assumes you went there

## Integration with Other Systems

### Ship Requisition
- Reputation from missions is primary source for ship upgrades
- See SHIP_SYSTEM.md for ship reputation requirements
- Example: Need 500 rep for Constellation (40-50 missions)

### Character Progression
- Experience from missions increases skills
- Higher skills = better success rates
- Rank promotions from XP unlock better ships
- See character skills in status display

### Faction Relations
- Missions completed increase Federation standing
- Some missions may affect other faction relations
- Maintains good standing for station access

### Captain's Log
- Mission acceptance logged automatically
- Success/failure recorded with stardate
- View full history in captain's log
- Useful for tracking career progression

## Mission Statistics
Track your performance:
- **Missions Completed**: Total successful missions
- **Active Missions**: Current missions in progress
- **Success Rate**: Calculate your completion percentage
- **Favorite Type**: Which mission types you complete most

## Troubleshooting

### "No missions available"
- Refresh the mission board
- You may be at a low level (fly to major systems)
- Galaxy needs nearby systems for mission generation

### "Insufficient skill"
- Train your character through gameplay
- Complete easier missions first
- Check which skill is required
- Some skills may need specific gameplay to improve

### "Maximum active missions reached"
- Complete one of your 3 active missions
- Or fail a mission to free a slot
- Cannot accept more than 3 at once

### Mission not appearing on board
- Check if you already accepted it
- Look in "Active Missions" section
- Refresh may have cycled it out
- Generate new missions by refreshing
