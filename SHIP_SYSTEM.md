# Ship Requisition System - Guide

## Overview
The new ship requisition system allows you to progress through 24 different canonical Federation starships, from the humble Miranda-class to the mighty Enterprise-J Universe-class dreadnought.

## How It Works

### Starting Ship
**All captains now start with a Miranda-class light cruiser**, regardless of species. This is your entry-level command, and you'll need to earn your way up through the ranks and reputation to command larger vessels.

### Reputation Points
Reputation points are earned through various activities:

**Exploration:**
- Discovering new systems: +5 reputation
- First visit to canonical systems: +10 reputation

**Combat:**
- Defeating Scout ships: +5 reputation
- Defeating Frigates: +10 reputation
- Defeating Cruisers: +20 reputation
- Defeating Battleships: +30 reputation
- Defeating Dreadnoughts: +50 reputation
- Diplomatic combat resolution: +25 reputation

**Diplomacy:**
- Trade agreements: +10 reputation
- Major alliances: +50 reputation
- Successful first contact: +30 reputation

**Experience Bonus:**
- You also gain reputation equal to 50% of experience earned

### Rank Requirements
Each ship has a minimum rank requirement:
- **Rank 0 (Lieutenant Commander)**: Miranda, Oberth
- **Rank 1 (Lieutenant Junior Grade)**: Constitution
- **Rank 2 (Lieutenant)**: Constitution Refit, Saber
- **Rank 3 (Lieutenant Commander)**: Excelsior, Ambassador, Steamrunner, Norway
- **Rank 4 (Commander)**: Intrepid, Nebula, Akira
- **Rank 5 (Captain)**: Defiant, Galaxy
- **Rank 6 (Fleet Captain)**: Sovereign, Prometheus
- **Rank 7 (Commodore)**: Luna, Vesta
- **Rank 8 (Rear Admiral)**: Odyssey, Venture, Excelsior Refit
- **Rank 10 (Vice Admiral)**: Universe, Yorktown
- **Rank 11 (Admiral)**: Enterprise-J

## How to Requisition a Ship

1. **Open Ship Requisition Menu**
   - From the ship command menu, select option 7
   
2. **View Available Ships**
   - Ships are organized by tier
   - [CURRENT] = Your current ship
   - [AVAILABLE] = You can afford and meet rank requirements
   - [LOCKED] = Shows how much more reputation you need

3. **Select a Ship**
   - Enter the number of the ship you want
   - Review the comparison with your current ship
   - Confirm the purchase

4. **Name Your Ship**
   - Choose from suggested names or create your own
   - Ship will be assigned a new NCC registry

5. **Transfer Complete**
   - All crew, resources, and cargo transfer automatically
   - Crew morale gets a boost for upgrades
   - Gain Command experience for taking a new command

## Strategy Tips

### Early Game (Miranda to Excelsior)
- Focus on exploration to build reputation steadily
- Fight smaller enemies you can handle
- Use diplomacy when possible for big reputation gains

### Mid Game (Intrepid to Galaxy)
- Balance combat, exploration, and diplomacy
- Take on bigger enemies for larger reputation rewards
- Build alliances with major factions

### Late Game (Sovereign and beyond)
- You'll need 2000+ reputation for elite ships
- At Rank 8+, focus on diplomatic victories (+50 rep each)
- Combat against Battleships/Dreadnoughts gives 30-50 rep

## Transfer Process
When you acquire a new ship:
- All dilithium and provisions transfer
- Key crew officers transfer
- Ship retains current location
- Crew morale increases (for upgrades)
- Old ship is "reassigned" in the fleet

## Reputation Farming Tips

**Best Reputation Per Hour:**
1. **Diplomatic Alliances** - 50 rep each (but requires high relations)
2. **First Contact Missions** - 30 rep each (find inhabited systems)
3. **Combat + Diplomacy** - Fight, then negotiate for 20-30 rep
4. **System Discovery** - 5-10 rep each (explore methodically)

**Fastest Path to Ultimate Ships:**
- Need 10,000 reputation for Enterprise-J
- Also need Rank 11 (Fleet Admiral) = 10,000 experience
- Estimated: 50-80 hours of gameplay
- Focus on high-value diplomatic missions and major combats

## FAQ

**Q: Can I go back to an old ship?**
A: No, ship transfers are permanent. Choose wisely!

**Q: What happens to my old ship?**
A: It's reassigned to another Starfleet captain in the lore.

**Q: Do I lose anything when transferring?**
A: No! All resources, crew, and location transfer automatically.

**Q: Can I rename my ship later?**
A: Not currently, but you can acquire the same class again with a new name.

**Q: What's the best ship overall?**
A: Enterprise-J for endgame, but Sovereign offers the best balance of accessibility and power.

**Q: Should I save up for higher tiers or buy intermediate ships?**
A: Buy intermediate ships! The stat boosts help you earn reputation faster.
